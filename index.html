Time of Day

A JavaScript Project by Victor Ulloa

Greeting goes here!

This webpage greets users with an appropriate salutation based on the time of day (morning, afternoon, evening, or night) and displays a corresponding image and background color. The greeting and visuals dynamically change depending on the current time, providing a personalized experience for users at different times of the day.
